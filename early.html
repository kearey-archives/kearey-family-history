III. The Early Generations: Luke Keary of Martry and His Descendants (c.1730–1857)

Luke Keary (c.1731–1806) of Martry is secured by the Martry headstone naming wife Elizabeth (alias Rooney), daughter Anne, and son Thomas (who died 1857). The inscription’s quality implies means and schooling (⁵).

Thomas Keary (c.1766–1857) relocates to Durhamstown/Liscarton; Griffith’s Valuation records a house, offices, and c.13½ acres at Liscarton under the Earl of Essex (⁶). Revision Books annotate the transfer “Dead → to James Keary,” proving succession (⁷). O’Hart’s pedigree lists sons Luke, Hugh, James, echoed in later civil records.

1821 Dormstown households reflect Thomas/Anne with children Luke, Thomas, Anne, Bridget, in the orbit of later Durhamstown records; this fixes the geography ahead of civil registration (⁸).
