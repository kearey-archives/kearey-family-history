Overview. Project Scope and Method

This edition reconstructs the Keary / Kearey families of County Meath (c.1730–1969) from primary records—headstones, civil registrations, valuation books, parish registers, and the 1821/1901/1911 censuses—integrating each citation into a continuous narrative with confidence grading. The county line (Martry → Durhamstown/Liscarton) is developed alongside a Betaghstown–Bohermeen collateral cluster and a Dublin–London artisan offshoot established by Thomas Kearey (baptised 1791) who removed to London c.1816.
