IX. Socio‑economic, Religious & Cultural Commentary

Meath’s mixed‑farming economy and tenant‑purchase reforms underpinned continuity in Durhamstown/Ongenstown; metropolitan artisanal ladders and wartime service shaped the London branch. Burial clustering at Bohermeen (Plot B‑294) evidences twentieth‑century cohesion.
