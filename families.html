VI. Associated Families & Proven Parentage

Ongenstown births confirm James Keary & Bridget (“Biddy”) Coyle as parents of Luke (1866) (⁸), Patt (1868) (⁹), Hugh (1870) (¹⁰), and Bridget (1873) (¹¹). Luke Keary married Mary Leonard (1886/7) (¹²), daughter of Nicholas Leonard (¹³). Children include William (1888) (¹⁴), Patrick (1893) (¹⁵), and James (1897) (¹⁶); William married Elizabeth Smith (1925) (¹⁷). Mary (Leonard) Keary died 1935 (¹⁸); Luke has a 1938 index variant as “Carey” (¹⁹). Rose Keary died 1921 (²⁰).
