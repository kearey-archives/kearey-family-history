VIII. Dublin & London Offshoot (1791–Present)

Thomas Kearey (bap. 1791) migrated to London c.1816 and remained. His records show baggage porter → gold refiner → goldsmith. The spelling Kearey stabilised in London clerical usage; the offshoot continues via Terence Arthur Kearey (b. 1935).

Line: Thomas (1791) → Thomas Henry (1820–1867) m. Hannah Raybould → Alfred (1854–1917) m. Martha Sutton → Albert Edward (1889–1971) m. Elsie May Collins → Terence Arthur (b.1935).
