Overview. Purpose & Scope

Purpose. This digital edition assembles the primary evidence for the Keary / Kearey families of County Meath, 1730–1969, and interprets that evidence in its social and economic setting. It draws on civil registrations, censuses, valuation records, parish registers, headstones, and local histories. Each intergenerational claim is stated with documentary footing and graded confidence.

Scope. The reconstruction follows the core Meath tenancy from Martry to Durhamstown/Liscarton, documents collateral households in the Betaghstown–Bohermeen orbit, and traces the Dublin–London artisan offshoot founded by Thomas Kearey (baptised 1791) who left Ireland c.1816 and whose line continues through Terence Arthur Kearey (b. 1935).
