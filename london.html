VIII. Dublin & London Offshoot (1791–Present)

Thomas Kearey (bapt. 1791) moved to London c.1816, recorded as baggage porter → gold refiner → goldsmith. The Kearey spelling stabilised in London clerical practice. Direct line: Thomas (1791) → Thomas Henry (1820–1867) m. Hannah Raybould → Alfred (1854–1917) m. Martha Sutton (WWI casualties within family) → Albert Edward (1889–1971) m. Elsie May Collins (1933) → Terence Arthur (b.1935).
